# IDEA PARKING LOT

New ideas enter here before they enter code. Parking an idea does not approve it, schedule
it, promise it, or justify supporting architecture. Promotion requires evidence, placement
in PRD/ROADMAP, an approved plan, and an ADR when the decision is expensive to reverse.

## Experience candidates

- Advanced hand gestures beyond the one action selected by V2.5 evidence
- Spatial audio
- Eye tracking
- Narrative timeline
- Additional room templates
- WebXR Viewer Client experiment (only under the ROADMAP XR-1 triggers)
- Vision Pro evaluation
- Native VR shell (only under the ROADMAP XR-2 triggers)

## Creation and discovery candidates

- **Photograph Space home:** an editorial entry surface for owner-approved featured
  exhibitions, collections, and photographers. It requires an explicit public-visibility
  and moderation model; unlisted V5 publications must never appear automatically.
- **Lumina Curator:** a supervised curation assistant that proposes controlled-template
  arrangements with reasons, previews, and reversible choices. The photographer approves
  every persisted change; the agent cannot publish, delete, invite, or freely place XYZ
  objects. Consider only after V5 closes and the current editor/scene evidence is reviewed.
- **Permissioned curator-agent collaboration:** only after real V6 human collaboration
  exists. Agent discovery or A2A-style exchange must be user-invited, scoped, auditable,
  and unable to expose private photographs. No autonomous open-Internet social agent.
- Portfolio analytics
- Public discovery and SEO expansion
- Exhibition duplication
- Template marketplace
- Visitor-to-photographer feedback
- Exhibition reactions or comments
- Return-visitor and narrative-path insights

## Product polish (parked during V3–V5 field runs, 2026-07-16)

- Library empty-state hint explaining that photographs are used by the exhibition
  editor (owner confusion report: "isn't this an exhibition?")
- `beforeunload` guard for unsaved editor keystrokes inside the autosave debounce window
- Per-byte upload progress (XHR) once photograph sizes warrant it
- EXIF extraction with GPS stripped/hidden by default, when a product surface needs it
- Copy-link button beside the public URL in the editor

## Commercial candidates

- Custom domains
- Brand themes
- Usage and storage plans
- Enterprise approval flows and SSO
- Digital ownership or NFT integration

## Promotion checklist

- [ ] A named user and problem exist.
- [ ] Evidence shows the problem matters now.
- [ ] The idea supports the Product Principles.
- [ ] Its active milestone, Product Goal, and Non-goals are approved.
- [ ] Dependencies and architecture boundaries are understood.
- [ ] Success and removal criteria are measurable.
